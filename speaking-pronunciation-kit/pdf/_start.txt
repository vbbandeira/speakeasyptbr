Why pronunciation

actually matters

  You can have the right words and still not be
  understood. In Brazilian Portuguese, how you say it
  matters as much as what you say.

The 3 reasons most English speakers plateau

  They learn words, not sounds
  Vocabulary gets you started. Pronunciation

  gets you understood -- and keeps the

  conversation going.

  They don't train their ear
  If you can't hear the difference between "pķ"

  and "pĻ", you'll never say it right. This kit fixes

  that.

  They practise silently
  Reading doesn't build muscle memory.

  Speaking out loud -- even alone -- does. Every

  drill in this kit is designed to be said, not just

  read.

 THE SHIFT

     You don't need a perfect accent. You need an
     intelligible one -- where Brazilians understand you
     the first time, without asking you to repeat.

in 14 days

  This is not a magic pill. It's a focused sprint. 10 minutes
  a day, done consistently, will change how you sound in
  real conversations.

How to use

the kit

10 minutes Ę every day Ę out loud

  The kit has three parts that work together. Use all three
  to get the full effect -- skipping the audio is like going
  to the gym and not lifting anything.

Your daily 10-minute routine

    1 Open the e-book to today's chapter. Read the
         explanation -- it takes 2 min.

    2 Find the audio file for today's module. Press play.

              Slow version first -- listen without repeating

    3 Repeat out loud with the audio -- slow, then
         natural speed.

              Record yourself on your phone and compare

    4 Do the word drill in the e-book. 5Ł8 words, out
         loud, twice.

    5 Pick 1 phrase from the chapter. Use it today -- in
         a message or out loud to yourself.

  Audio files are numbered. Each chapter in the e-book

  references its files directly -- e.g. 03_stress-
  demo_1.mp3 . Keep your audio folder open alongside

  the e-book.

 THE ONE RULE

     Never read a drill silently. If your mouth isn't
     moving, you're not practising pronunciation --
     you're just reading. Say every single word out loud.
