Why pronunciation
actually matters

01

You can have the right words and still not be understood. In Brazilian Portuguese, how you say it matters as much as what you say.

The 3 reasons most English speakers plateau
🧠
They learn words, not sounds Vocabulary gets you started. Pronunciation gets you understood — and keeps the conversation going.
👂
They don't train their ear If you can't hear the difference between "pé" and "pê", you'll never say it right. This kit fixes that.
🎯
They practise silently Reading doesn't build muscle memory. Speaking out loud — even alone — does. Every drill in this kit is designed to be said, not just read.
💡
The shift
You don't need a perfect accent. You need an intelligible one — where Brazilians understand you the first time, without asking you to repeat.
📄 PAGE 03 — What to Expect in 14 Days
Start Here · 02

What to expect
in 14 days

02

This is not a magic pill. It's a focused sprint. 10 minutes a day, done consistently, will change how you sound in real conversations.

By the end of day 14, you'll be able to:
✓
Pronounce the nasal sounds (ão, em, ãe) without stopping to think
✓
Use the Brazilian R — including at the start of words and in "rr"
✓
Sound natural with T/D before "i" (tia, dia, dinheiro)
✓
Link words together the way natives actually speak
✓
Say 50 survival phrases with natural rhythm and stress
✓
Hear the difference between sounds you couldn't distinguish before
📅
Realistic expectation
Days 1–7 feel like work. Days 8–14 feel like it's clicking. The goal isn't perfection — it's consistency that compounds.
📄 PAGE 04 — How to Use the Kit (with Audio)
Start Here · 03

How to use
the kit

10 minutes · every day · out loud

03

The kit has three parts that work together. Use all three to get the full effect — skipping the audio is like going to the gym and not lifting anything.

Your daily 10-minute routine
1
Open the e-book to today's chapter. Read the explanation — it takes 2 min.
2
Find the audio file for today's module. Press play.
🎧 Slow version first — listen without repeating
3
Repeat out loud with the audio — slow, then natural speed.
🎙️ Record yourself on your phone and compare
4
Do the word drill in the e-book. 5–8 words, out loud, twice.
5
Pick 1 phrase from the chapter. Use it today — in a message or out loud to yourself.
📁
Audio files are organized in folders by chapter. Each chapter in the e-book references its files directly — e.g. Chapter_01_Rhythm_Stress/Lesson_01.mp3. Keep your audio folder open alongside the e-book.
🔑
The one rule
Never read a drill silently. If your mouth isn't moving, you're not practising pronunciation — you're just reading. Say every single word out loud.
