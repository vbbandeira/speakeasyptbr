The main e-book. 100 everyday phrases across 10 chapters, with pronunciation cues and real-world context. Read cover-to-cover in about an hour. This is your primary read.

13-page bonus guide. The unwritten rules textbooks don't teach: kiss greetings, Pix, tipping, food culture, time norms, regional character, holidays. Read after the main e-book or anytime before a real Brazilian situation.

A 30-minute first session

03

You don't need a plan. You need a first 30 minutes. Here's exactly that.

1️⃣
Open Everyday Brazilian Portuguese.pdf
Start at the beginning. Read the introduction chapter — it sets up how the phrasebook works. ~5 minutes.
2️⃣
Read Chapter 1 — Greetings
Say each phrase out loud as you read. Don't memorise, just feel the shapes. ~10 minutes.
3️⃣
If you have the audio tier — play Chapter 1's audio folder
Listen to Lessons 001-010 once each, reading along in the PDF. Imitate Layla's rhythm. ~10 minutes.
4️⃣
Open the Brazilian Culture & Etiquette Guide — read page 4 (the beijo)
Just one chapter. Come back for more when you're planning a real Brazilian interaction. ~5 minutes.
🎯
The rule of first sessions
Don't try to finish the whole kit today. The main e-book is designed to be worked through one chapter at a time across multiple sittings. Consistency beats intensity. Come back tomorrow for Chapter 2.
