Your first words in Brazilian Portuguese

05

Brazilians are warm and expressive — a good greeting goes a long way. Learn these and you'll feel at home from day one.

🇧🇷 Portuguese (PT-BR) 🇺🇸 English
Oi!
Hi! Very casual, used among friends
Olá!
Hello! Neutral, works in any context
Bom dia!
Good morning!
Boa tarde!
Good afternoon!
Boa noite!
Good evening / night Used as both greeting and goodbye at night
Tudo bem?
How are you? Lit. "All good?" — very common
Tudo certo?
Everything ok?
E você?
And you?
Prazer em te conhecer!
Nice to meet you!
Com licença.
Excuse me To get attention or pass by someone
💡
Quick tip Brazilians often reply to "Tudo bem?" with "Tudo bem!" — it's both the question and the answer. Think of it like saying "How's it going?" / "Good, good!"
