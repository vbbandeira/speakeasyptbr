📄 PAGE 02 — How to use
How to use

Cut. Flip. Remember.

The flashcard method

02

Each page holds two cards. The dashed line between them is a cut line — or fold, if you prefer to keep the book intact.

✂️
Three ways to use
Print & cut — print the PDF, cut along the dashed lines, carry a deck of 202 physical cards.

Screen swipe — read on your phone or tablet. The Portuguese term is always at the top of each card; cover the bottom half with your hand to self-test.

Fold it — fold each page along the dashed line and review front-then-back like a classic card.
📚 What's on each card
Front — the Portuguese term, with a theme icon and level chip.
Back — the meaning/explanation in English.
Source — which dialogue introduced the term, so you can re-listen in context.
🧠
Spaced repetition tip
Review a card, then test yourself again one day later, three days later, and a week later. Memory loves spacing, not cramming.
