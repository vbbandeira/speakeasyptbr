📄 PAGE 02 — How to use
How to use

Cut. Flip. Remember.

The flashcard method

02

Each page holds four cards. The dashed lines are cut guides — or fold lines, if you prefer to keep the book intact.

✂️
Three ways to use
Print & cut — print the PDF, cut along dashed lines, carry a deck of 341 physical cards.

Screen swipe — read on your phone. Cover the meaning with your thumb to self-test from the Portuguese term.

Mix & review — shuffle cards, keep tricky ones in a "review again" pile, retire the ones you own.
📚 What's on each card
Emoji — a visual hook tied to the meaning, not the dialogue setting.
Term — the Portuguese word or expression.
Meaning — the English translation or explanation.
Source — the dialogue where it appeared, so you can re-listen in context.
🧠
Spaced repetition
Review a card, test yourself again one day later, three days later, and a week later. Memory loves spacing, not cramming.
