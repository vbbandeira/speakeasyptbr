The transcripts workflow

02

These transcripts are companions to your audio files. Use them to unlock comprehension you couldn't catch on passive listening.

🎧
The 3-pass workflow
Pass 1 — listen to the dialogue without the transcript. Get the rhythm, even if you miss words.

Pass 2 — read along with the transcript. Check vocab notes for tricky phrases.

Pass 3 — listen again, transcript closed. You'll catch more than before.
📚 What each section contains
Scene — the full Portuguese dialogue with speaker labels (A / B).
Vocab notes — tricky phrases, idioms, and Brazilianisms explained in English.
Comprehension questions — test your understanding after listening.
Fill-the-gap — from Plus tier onwards, word-completion exercises.
💡 Don't translate word-by-word
Let context teach you. You don't need to understand every word — you need to feel the rhythm of real Brazilian speech.

Dialogue 01

Pedindo um Uber

Ordering an Uber

05
Level A1
Setting Street / Phone
Theme Transportation
A
PassageiroOi, você é o motorista do Uber?
B
MotoristaSou sim! Você é o Marcos?
A
Sou eu. Tudo bem?
B
Tudo ótimo. Pode entrar. Você vai pra Avenida Paulista, né?
A
Isso, número 1500.
B
Beleza. Uns vinte minutos, mais ou menos.
A
Tá bom, obrigado.
B
Pode deixar o ar ligado ou tá bom assim?
A
Assim tá ótimo, obrigado.
B
Tranquilo. Boa noite!
📚 Vocab notes
Sou sim — emphatic "yes, I am" (common Brazilian pattern: verb + sim)
Pode entrar — "come in" / "go ahead and get in"
né? — short for "não é?" — tag question, like "right?"
Beleza — "cool" / "alright" (very common informal agreement)
Pode deixar — "shall I leave..." / "want me to keep..."
🎯 Comprehension questions
Where is the passenger going?
How long will the trip take approximately?
What does the driver offer to do?

Quanto fica? — "How much does it come to?"
Pode aproximar — "you can tap" (contactless payment)
