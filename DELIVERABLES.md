## Product 3 — Speaking & Pronunciation Kit

### Target customer
Intermediate learners (A2–B1+) who can already form sentences but don't sound Brazilian. Wants focused pronunciation training.

### Core promise
*"Master the 8 sounds that separate 'knowing the words' from 'sounding Brazilian' — guttural R, nasals, LH/NH, T/D palatalization, final L/S, connected speech."*

### Tier structure — all tiers blocked

#### €29.90 — Basic  🟡 BLOCKED ON LAYLA AUDIO
The core pedagogical product: PDF + all drill audios.

**Planned files**:
| File | State | What it is |
|---|---|---|
| `Start Here.pdf` | ✅ Ready | 4-page onboarding — why pronunciation matters, daily routine, file organisation |
| `Books/Speaking and Pronunciation Kit.pdf` | ✅ Ready | 28-page main guide. 8 chapters × 2–3 pages each: theory spotlight, comparison table, drill progression (5 levels), minimal pairs lab, common traps, cultural context. Plus intro "Sound Map", 50 Survival Phrases bonus, 14-Day Plan, Audio Index. |
| `Audio Drills/Chapter_01_Rhythm_Stress/` … `Chapter_08_Connected_Speech/` | 🟡 Reference AI audio in place; Layla pending | 46 audio drills total (per-lesson: natural utterance → slow repetition → English meaning) |
| `Audio Drills/Bonus_50_Survival_Phrases/` | 🟡 Reference AI audio in place; Layla pending | 50 survival phrases grouped into 13 audio files |

#### €69.90 — Plus  ⚪ NOT STARTED
Basic + 3 substantial extras.
- **Minimal Pair Flashcards** (Plus-tier-specific, different from BLL's Vocabulary Flashcards) — ~80–120 cards contrasting sounds like casa/caça, pão/pau, mau/mal
- **Top 50 Mistakes for English Speakers PDF** — e.g., "pressing T/D where Brazilians palatalize," "trilling R where a real Brazilian gutturalizes"
- **Pronunciation Quick-Reference Sheet** — 1-page laminate-able cheat sheet

#### €129.90 — Pro  ⚪ NOT STARTED
Plus + 3 advanced extras.
- **Speaking Scripts Pack** — 200 mini-scripts across scenarios (ordering food, airport, doctor, rental, job interview, etc.)
- **21-Day Fluency Plan PDF** — day-by-day routine combining the Kit + scripts + recording yourself
- **Regional Accent Guide PDF** — carioca vs paulista vs nordestino vs mineiro vs sulista — recognise which Brazilian accent you're hearing

### Why the audio pivoted from AI to human
Pronunciation is the literal product. ElevenLabs v2 multilingual TTS produced ~5–10% words with meaning-changing errors (e.g., "casa" pronounced "caça" = hunting). In a kit teaching exact pronunciation, each error corrodes trust. Layla (native Brazilian, the user's wife) records the ~1–2 hours of audio content manually for guaranteed quality.

AI is retained for non-critical content (stories, dialogues, immersion) — which is why the Listening Lab ships today while this Kit is blocked.

## File naming conventions

### Customer-facing (PDFs)
Title-case with natural spaces and ampersands. No underscores. Examples:
- `Everyday Brazilian Portuguese.pdf`
- `Brazilian Culture & Etiquette Guide.pdf`
- `Speaking & Pronunciation Kit.pdf`
- `Speaking & Pronunciation Kit - Start Here.pdf`
- `One Day in Brazil - Transcript.pdf`
- `Transcripts.pdf` / `Comprehension Workbook.pdf` / `Vocabulary Flashcards.pdf` (per-tier, inside `Books/`)

### Customer-facing (audio)
Title_Case with underscores (folder-friendly, visually cleaner in deeply-nested paths):
- **Pronunciation Kit:** `Chapter_01_Rhythm_Stress/Lesson_01.mp3` … `Lesson_07.mp3`
- **Listening Lab:** `Dialogue_01.mp3` … `Dialogue_50.mp3` (+ `_slow` variants)
- **Everyday:** `Chapter 01 - Greetings and First Impressions/Lesson 001 - <phrase>.mp3` (natural spaces are acceptable here because the Audio Book path depth is shallow)

### Developer-facing (HTML source, scripts)
snake_case. Not shipped to customer.
