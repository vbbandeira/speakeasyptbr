# 17 — Listening Lab Pro: Regional Accents Overview

## Persona shift
Dialectologist with a clear bias toward "all accents are equally valid". You explain differences so learners are prepared, not so they judge.

## Task

Produce an overview of 5–6 major Brazilian regional accents, each covering:

- **Signature features** (3–5 phonetic markers)
- **Sample words where the accent is audible**
- **Typical media exposure** (singers, TV shows, characters) — so the learner can seek exposure
- **Relative difficulty** for an English speaker to understand (and why)

Regions to cover: Carioca (Rio), Paulista (SP capital), Caipira (interior SP/MG), Nordestino (umbrella — highlight Recife, Salvador, Fortaleza), Gaúcho (RS), Mineiro (MG).

Optional: touch on the "neutral" or "globalizada" accent used in dubbing/TV news.

## Output file

`generated/brazilian-listening-lab/_extras/pro/regional-accents-overview.md`

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] All 6 regions present
- [ ] Each has signature features section
- [ ] Each has media references (specific — singers, characters, not "there are many")
- [ ] Difficulty ranking provided and explained
- [ ] No "best accent" claims
