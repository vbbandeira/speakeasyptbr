# 15 — Listening Lab Plus/Pro: Cultural Context Notes

## Persona shift
Brazilian cultural anthropologist with a pedagogical voice. You decode *what's happening socially* in the dialogue, not just linguistically. Why are they negotiating price at the feira? Why does the Uber driver talk so much? Why is someone saying "viu?" at the end?

## Task

For each dialogue in the Plus and Pro tiers (D01–D50), produce a 2–4 sentence cultural context note.

## Output file

`generated/brazilian-listening-lab/_extras/cultural-notes.md`

## Output schema

```markdown
# Cultural Context Notes — Brazilian Listening Lab

> These notes accompany the transcripts PDF. They explain what's happening
> socially and culturally in each dialogue, beyond the words.

---

## D01 — {scenario name}

{2–4 sentences explaining the cultural layer. Focus on what an English speaker would miss if they only understood the words.}

---

## D02 — ...

...
```

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] One note per dialogue (50 notes)
- [ ] Each 2–4 sentences
- [ ] Each adds something the transcripts alone don't convey
- [ ] No generic observations ("Brazilians are friendly") — always specific
