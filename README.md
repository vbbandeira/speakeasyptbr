# SpeakEasy Portuguese

> Practical language products for English speakers who want to start speaking Brazilian Portuguese — today.

---

## About

SpeakEasy Portuguese creates focused, no-fluff learning materials designed around real usage. No grammar overload. No textbook format. Just the phrases, sounds, and habits that actually move the needle.

---

## Projects

### ✅ 01 — 100 Everyday Brazilian Portuguese Phrases
> *Folder: `/100-phrases`*

A phrasebook-style e-book with 100 everyday PT-BR phrases organized into 10 chapters, with English translations, contextual notes, and a built-in 11-day practice plan.

**Format:** PDF (designed in Canva, prototyped in HTML/CSS)

**What's inside:**
- 10 thematic chapters (greetings → transport)
- PT-BR + English side by side
- Contextual usage notes per phrase
- Quick Practice Plan (11 days, 1 chapter/day)
- Next Steps page pointing to the Audio Kit

**Products:**
| Version | Price | Includes |
|---------|-------|----------|
| E-book | €9 | PDF phrasebook |
| Bundle | €19 | E-book + Audio Kit |

**Status:** 🟡 Final review — pending cover assets & CTA link

### 🔜 02 — Speaking & Pronunciation Kit
> *Folder: `/pronunciation-kit`*

The natural next step after the 100 Phrases e-book. Focused on the sounds and rhythm that make PT-BR feel natural to English ears.

**Planned format:** Audio files + companion PDF script

**Planned contents:**
- Audio drills (slow + natural speed) for all 100 phrases
- 14-day pronunciation plan (10–15 min/day)
- Mini listening pack
- Bonus real-life conversation scripts

**Status:** 🔵 In development
